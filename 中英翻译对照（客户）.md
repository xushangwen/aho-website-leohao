关于澳弘 - 发展历程 
澳弘成立 (1973-1999)  AOH was established (1973-1999) 

1973 - 公司初创
创建"武进县孝都公社长江标牌厂"，开始生产简易的枊器、铭牌、五金等产品起步。
1973 - Company Inception
Founded as "Wujin County Xiaodu Commune Changjiang Label Factory," starting with the production of simple wickerwork, nameplates, and hardware products.

1982 - 开始生产单面PCB板
进入电子行业，为当时中国电视第一品牌，"黄河电视"供应单面PCB。
1982 - Entry into Single-Sided PCB Production
Entered the electronics industry by supplying single-sided PCBs for "Huanghe TV," the leading television brand in China at the time.
 
1995 - 年销售达千万元
当年总销售额突破1000万元人民币。
1995 - Annual Sales Reach 10 Million Yuan
Total annual sales surpassed 10 million RMB.
 
势不可挡 (2000-2019) Unstoppable (2000-2019)

2004 - 成立“海弘”
主营业务聚焦生产单面PCB板。年销售额突破1亿元人民币。
2004 - Establishment of "Haihong"
Main business focused on producing single-sided PCBs. Annual sales exceeded 100 million RMB.
 
2005 - 成立“澳弘”
●	开始生产双面和多层PCB板。
●	援建古巴PCB生产线。
●	成为LG绿色伙伴（GREEN PARTNER）。
●	公司首次通过ISO14000环境管理体系认证。
2005 - Establishment of "AOH"
●	Began production of double-sided and multilayer PCBs.
●	Assisted in the construction of a PCB production line in Cuba.
●	Become an LG Green Partner.
●	Achieved initial ISO 14001 Environmental Management System certification.
 
2009 - 扩建澳弘二期工厂
澳弘成立三年即实现满产，扩建二期生产车间，双面PCB板的年产能从30万平方米提高到80万平方米。
2009 - Expansion of AOH Phase II Factory
Aohong reached full production capacity within three years of its establishment, leading to the expansion of the Phase II production plant. The annual production capacity for double-sided PCBs increased from 300,000 square meters to 800,000 square meters.

2012 - 海弘整体搬迁
“海弘”整体搬迁到滨江工业园区的新基地，完成整厂自动化改造工程。单面PCB板的年产能300万平方米。
2012 - Haihong Relocation
"Haihong" completed a full relocation to a new base in the Binjiang Industrial Park, along with a comprehensive factory automation upgrade. The annual production capacity for single-sided PCBs reached 3 million square meters.

2017 - 成立香港子公司
在香港成立第一家海外子公司，Elite Prospect Trading Limited （香港昇耀）, 开始走向国际化经营。
2017 - Establishment of Hong Kong Subsidiary
Established the first overseas subsidiary, Elite Prospect Trading Limited (Hong Kong), marking the beginning of international operations.

2018 - 完成股份制改造成立新加坡子公司
●	2018年2月，在新加坡成立子公司，新加坡Elite Prospect Singapore Pte. Ltd., 进一步开拓海外市场。
●	2018年12月公司完成股份制改造，
●	澳弘、海弘、香港昇耀、新加坡ELITE合并成立股份制公司，母公司为“常州澳弘电子股份有限公司”。
2018 - Completion of Joint-Stock Reform & Establishment of Singapore Subsidiary
●	In February 2018, established a subsidiary in Singapore, Elite Prospect Singapore Pte. Ltd., to further expand overseas markets. 
●	In December 2018, the company completed its joint-stock reform.
●	Aohong, Haihong, Hong Kong Shengyao, and Singapore ELITE were merged to form a joint-stock company, with the parent company being "Changzhou Aohong Electronics Co., Ltd."

2022 - 澳弘A2生产基地投产
●	澳弘新建完成A2工厂的"年产120万平方米高精密度多层板、高密度互连积层板建设项目"。
●	海弘新建完成"年产60万平米单面板工厂建设项目",总年产能达到560万平方米。
2022 - Aohong A2 Production Base Commences Operation
●	Aohong completed the construction of the "Annual 1.2 Million Square Meters High-Precision Multilayer Board and High-Density Interconnect (HDI) Build-Up Board Project" at the new A2 plant.

Aohong commenced construction on the "Annual 600,000 Square Meters High-Density Interconnect Printed Circuit Board Project," achieving full capacity investment for the Aohong A2 plant.
